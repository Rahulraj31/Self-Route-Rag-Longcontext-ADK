# GlobalCorp Internal Policy: Paid Time Off (PTO) and Leave
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. STANDARD PTO ACCRUAL
Full-time salaried employees accrue 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year, accrued at a rate of 1.66 days per month. 
Employees with 5+ years of tenure accrue 25 days of PTO annually.

2. SICK LEAVE
GlobalCorp provides unlimited sick leave for physical and mental health recovery. If an employee takes more than 5 consecutive days of sick leave, a doctor's note must be submitted to the HR Leave Administrator.

4. OTHER LEAVE TYPES
For information regarding Parental Leave, Bereavement Leave, Sabbaticals, and Company Holidays, please refer to the comprehensive Leave Handbook available on the GlobalCorp Intranet. This document serves only as a brief summary of Standard PTO.
