# GlobalCorp Internal Policy: Parental Leave Appendix (Supplement to Handbook)
Effective Date: March 2026

1. HOLIDAYS DURING LEAVE
If a scheduled GlobalCorp holiday (e.g., New Year's Day, Independence Day) falls within an employee's parental leave period, the leave duration is automatically extended by one business day.

2. NEW PARENT SUPPORT BOX
Post-birth or post-adoption, all employees are eligible for a "New Parent Support Box" (valued at $200). This contains GlobalCorp branded baby gear and essential supplies. 
Request Process: Submit a request via the "Family First" portal within 60 days of the child's arrival.

3. CHILDCARE SUBSIDY
For remote and office-based employees with children under the age of 5, GlobalCorp provides a monthly childcare subsidy of $500 per child. This is paid out as a taxable benefit through payroll.

4. EMERGENCY LEAVE FOR PARENTS
In addition to standard parental leave, parents are granted 5 days of "Emergency Family Leave" per year to handle unexpected childcare issues or medical emergencies involving their children. This does not count against PTO or Sick Leave.
