# GlobalCorp Internal Policy: Code of Conduct and Workplace Behavior
Effective Date: March 1, 2024

1. ANTI-HARASSMENT AND DISCRIMINATION
GlobalCorp maintains a zero-tolerance policy against harassment, discrimination, or bullying based on race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, or genetic information. 

2. REPORTING INCIDENTS
Employees who witness or experience violations of the Code of Conduct should immediately report the incident through one of the following channels:
a. Their direct manager or department head.
b. Their designated HR Business Partner (HRBP).
c. The anonymous GlobalCorp Ethics Hotline, available 24/7 at 1-800-555-0199 or online at ethics.globalcorp.internal.

3. NON-RETALIATION
GlobalCorp strictly prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports an incident in good faith or participates in an investigation. Any employee found engaging in retaliatory behavior will face immediate termination.

4. CONFLICT OF INTEREST
Employees must disclose any relationships, investments, or outside business activities that could inappropriately influence their decisions at GlobalCorp. This includes accepting gifts over $50 from vendors or clients. All disclosures must be logged in the "Conflict of Interest Portal" within Workday.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
All internal strategies, financial data, and customer information are strictly confidential. Discussing unreleased products in public forums, social media, or with family members is prohibited. Violations of the Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) may result in legal action.
